一、Demo(示例)01 -- <space>/<tab>/<enter>/<return>组合
\documentclass{article}
\begin{document}
	i love this    country\\
	but you don't like		this\\
	this is line one
	this is line two
\end{document}

内容讲解
任意单个或多个组合(只能为单个<enter>/<return>), 都作为一个<space>进行对待


二、Demo(示例)02 -- blank line
\documentclass{article}
\begin{document}
this is paragraph one

this is paragraph two
\end{document}

内容讲解
空白行用于段落的结束


三、Demo(示例)03 -- short space
\documentclass{article}
\begin{document}
    "'Fi' or 'fum'" he said

    "\,'Fi' or 'fum'\," he said

    " 'Fi' or 'fum' " he said
\end{document}

内容讲解
\,用于比<space>更短的空格, 用于分隔'与"


四、Demo(示例)04 -- 破折号
\documentclass{article}
\begin{document}
    An intra-word dash or hyphen, as in X-ray.

    A medium dash for number ranges, like 1--2.

    A punctuation dash---like this.
\end{document}

内容讲解
-用于表示连字符
--用于表示数字范围
---用于主句与从句


五、Demo{示例}05 -- 句子结束相关的空格
\documentclass{article}
\begin{document}
    this is the line onE. but nothing more\\
    this is the line onE.\ but nothing more\\
    this is the line onE\@. but nothing more\\
    this is the line one. but nothing more\\
    this is the line one.\ but nothing more\\
	this is the line (one.) but nothing more\\
	this is the line (one.)\ but nothing more
\end{document}

内容讲解
1.word之间的的空格为\<space>, 句子结束时的空格为<space>, \<space>的空间小于<space>
	空间大小: \,  <  \<space>  <  <space>
2.在.之前为大写字母时, 默认不是句子结束, 后续<space>默认为\<space>, 即单词之间的空格
3.当.之前为非大写字母时, 后续代表句子结束, 后续<space>默认为<space>. 可强制使用\<space>定义单词之间的空格
4.当.之前为大写字母时, 默认不是句子结束. 但可在.之前使用\@来指定.为句子结束标志
5.当.之后有)/'/"时，规则同.结尾
6.?/!/:效果同.效果一致
